== Description ==
[Solicitors Nationwide](http://www.solicitorsnationwide.co.uk/sncct)

The Solicitors Nationwide Conveyancing Comparison Tool allows you to provide conveyancing quotes from a selected panel of solicitors on your Wordpress blog.

For each instruction placed via your site you can earn between &pound;25 - &pound;100.

If you have a blog or site related to buying and selling property or providing financial advice this plugin will provide you with an extra source of income and an additional service to your visitors.

Please note this plugin is only applicable for the UK property market.

**Features**

* set your own commission levels (up to &pound;100 per instruction)
* once instruction is placed, clients receive updates via email and SMS text direct from solicitor
* provide substantially cheaper quotes than going direct to solicitor
* referral fees paid direct to the bank account or PayPal account of your choice
* large panel of conveyancing solicitors from around the UK

== Installation ==
Use the Wordpress installer or install manually:

1.    Download and unzip the plugin.
1.    Upload the solicitors-nationwide-conveyancing-comparison-tool directory to /wp-content/plugins/.
1.    Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress.
1.    Signup for your affiliate key at [Solicitors Nationwide](http://www.solicitorsnationwide.co.uk/wp-login.php?action=register)
1.    Once you have signed up, copy your Affiliate Key into the corresponding field above together with the email address you registered
1.    Set the commission levels you wish to achieve for each scenario
1.    Set the url where the plugin will operate from
1.    Place the [\'show_conveyancing\'] shortcode on a page of your choice.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Do I have to pay any fees on my commission? =
No, the amount you specify is the amount you will receive.

= When will I get paid? =
You will be paid by the 14th of each month for any instructions completed in the month previous.

= How long does it take to get paid from the instruction being placed? =
This depends on how quickly the transaction goes through.  If there is a short chain then the whole process can take as little as 6 weeks, for longer chains it can take up to and over 12 weeks.

= What happens if the property transaction falls through? =
If for any reason the property transaction does not proceed the instruction will be cancelled and no commission will be paid.
